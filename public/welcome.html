Welcome to OTOI 0.1.0!

Why Use OTOI?

OTOI is the only organizational tool you'll need for college. This tool combines everything you need to keep on track of assignments, classes, and exams. With to-do lists, a schedule viewer, and a place for notes, OTOI keeps you On Top Of It.

0.1.0 Features

  • To-Do List (2 minute learn)
    With OTOI's To-Do List, you can track all of your tasks for the rest of the term! Simply input the name of your task, its due date, and its "do" date -- that is, when you plan to do it! You can add new tasks and cross off the ones you complete as you go.
  • Scheduler (4 minute learn)
    OTOI's scheduler provides a clean and beautiful interface for you to view your weekly class schedule. Our schedule shows you all the information you need to know about your classes. Input the name, time, and location of each of your classes to view them here!
  • Files (2 minute learn)
    OTOI's file storage system means you can keep track of your notes at all times. Upload, download, or delete your class notes to make OTOI an all-in-one hub for your schoolwork.
